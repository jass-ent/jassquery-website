Common questions

Everything you need to know.

Do I still need a Keepa subscription?
For the free tier (BYOK), yes — you use your own Keepa API key. On all paid plans, a Keepa token allowance is bundled in. You may still want your own Keepa Pro subscription for browsing, but QueryForge handles all your automated query execution.
What is a "credit" and how do I know how many I'll use?
One credit covers a typical search returning around 10,000 ASINs — but result size is flexible. Run broad searches or narrow ones, your call. Larger result sets draw more credits; smaller ones stretch your budget further. When you save and schedule a query, QueryForge shows you the estimated credit cost — so there are never surprises. Your balance is always visible in your dashboard.
What does "no daily limits" actually mean?
Keepa's own Product Finder limits how many queries you can run per day. QueryForge gives you a monthly credit budget with zero per-day restrictions — run 1 query today and 50 tomorrow if you want. Your budget, your schedule.
How is QueryForge different from just using Keepa?
Keepa is a powerful data tool that requires manual operation. QueryForge is the automation layer on top — scheduling, executing, and delivering results automatically every day. It's the difference between a great car and a self-driving one.
What is "Run Now" and why does it matter?
Run Now lets you trigger any query immediately, jumping to the front of the execution queue. When you spot a trending niche or react to market news, you don't have to wait for tomorrow's scheduled run — you get fresh data within minutes. Free tier includes 1 Run Now per month; Starter 1×/week; Pro 3×/week; Power gets unlimited.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — no contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your account settings at any time and you'll retain access until the end of your billing period. We're confident you'll stay because the results speak for themselves.
Where do my results go?
Query results are delivered as a CSV file, dropped directly into the storage destination you configure during setup — either Google Drive or an Azure Blob Container. You choose one at setup; QueryForge writes every result file straight there. Your data stays in your account, in your storage — we never hold onto it.
