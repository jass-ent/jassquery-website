The Origin

Built by a seller,
for sellers.

JASS Query didn't come from a product team. It came from a frustrated Amazon reseller — one with eight years of sourcing experience and an obsessive relationship with Keepa's Product Finder.

The problems were loud and unanimous in the seller community: queries that vanished on a misclick, the same filter setup rebuilt from memory every morning, and a Product Finder that felt powerful but unforgiving. These weren't edge cases — they were the daily experience of tens of thousands of sellers trying to do the work.

So we built the tools we wished existed. Then we gave them to the community that asked for them first.
